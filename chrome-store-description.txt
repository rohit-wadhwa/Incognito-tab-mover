Incognito Tab Mover — Bidirectional tab mover: Normal ↔ Incognito.

⚠️ REQUIRED SETUP — PLEASE READ FIRST
This extension moves tabs into Incognito windows, so Chrome requires you to allow it there:
1) Open chrome://extensions
2) Find "Incognito Tab Mover" → click Details
3) Turn ON "Allow in incognito"
Without this, nothing will happen when you use the extension.

NOT WORKING / NO "ALLOW IN INCOGNITO" OPTION?
If that toggle is missing, or you see "Failed to create window: Incognito mode is disabled", then Incognito mode is disabled in your browser (the IncognitoModeAvailability policy — common on managed/work profiles). The extension cannot work until Incognito is enabled. On a work device, ask your IT admin, or use a personal Chrome profile. You can verify at chrome://policy or by pressing Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+N.

WHAT IT DOES
Move a single tab between Normal and Incognito with Alt+M (Option+M on Mac), or move all tabs at once with Alt+Shift+M (Option+Shift+M on Mac). URLs are moved without transferring session history, preserving your privacy.

• Individual tab transfer — Alt+M / Option+M
• Bulk transfer — Alt+Shift+M / Option+Shift+M
• Bidirectional: Normal → Incognito and Incognito → Normal
• Privacy first: only the URL is moved, not session history
• Simple popup with buttons for both actions

PRIVACY
No data collection. Everything happens locally in your browser.
