Incognito Tab Mover

Use Alt+M (Option+M on Mac)
Use Alt+Shift+M (Option+Shift+M on Mac)
Bidirectional Tab Movement:
  • From Normal window → moves to Incognito
  • From Incognito window → moves to Normal
⚠ Required setup: You must turn on “Allow in incognito” for this extension at chrome://extensions → Incognito Tab Mover → Details. Without it, the extension cannot open incognito windows and nothing will happen.
Don’t see the “Allow in incognito” toggle? Your browser or organization has disabled Incognito mode (IncognitoModeAvailability policy). The extension can’t work until Incognito is enabled — on a managed/work device ask your IT admin, or use a personal Chrome profile. Check via chrome://policy or press Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+N.
