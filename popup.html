Incognito Tab Mover

Use Alt+M (Option+M on Mac)
Use Alt+Shift+M (Option+Shift+M on Mac)
Bidirectional Tab Movement:
  • From Normal window → moves to Incognito
  • From Incognito window → moves to Normal

Note: Enable "Allow in incognito" in chrome://extensions/ for full functionality.
